Fee Policies

Monthly Payment Cycle

Fees are due at the start of each month. No advance payment required beyond one month at a time.

Free Demo Before Any Payment

You attend one complete free demo class first. Payment only begins after you're satisfied and choose to enroll.

Session Credit Policy

If a session is cancelled by Sir, it will be rescheduled at no extra charge or credited to next month's count.

Pause / Hold Option

Students can pause their enrollment for up to 30 days (exams, illness, travel) with prior notice. No fees charged during pause.

Sibling & Referral Discount

Enroll a sibling or refer a friend who joins — get ₹500 off your next month's fee. No limit on referrals.

Financial Hardship Consideration

Deserving students facing genuine financial difficulty are encouraged to speak with Sandeep Sir directly — case-by-case support is available.

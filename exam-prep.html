📍 NEET Preparation Roadmap

Phase 1 — Foundation (Months 1–3)

Complete NCERT mastery for Class 11 syllabus — Physical Chemistry basics, Mechanics, Thermodynamics. Build the base without shortcuts.

Phase 2 — Advanced Concepts (Months 4–7)

Class 12 syllabus — Organic Chemistry, Electrochemistry, Optics, Modern Physics. Application-level MCQ practice begins.

Phase 3 — Intensive Practice (Months 8–10)

Chapter-wise full tests, timed mock tests, weakness analysis, and targeted revision of low-scoring areas.

Phase 4 — Final Revision (Last 4–6 Weeks)

Full syllabus rapid revision, full-length mock tests under exam conditions, exam-day strategy and mental preparation.

📍 JEE Preparation Roadmap

Phase 1 — Concept Foundation (Months 1–4)

Build strong fundamentals in all three subjects. Mechanics, Algebra, Physical Chemistry — done properly with worked examples and concept clarity.

Phase 2 — Problem Solving (Months 5–8)

Progress to JEE-level problems. Calculus, Electricity & Magnetism, Organic Chemistry. Learn multiple approaches to the same problem.

Timed chapter tests, sectional mock tests, and full JEE Main-pattern papers. Analyze every mistake and eliminate patterns of error.

Phase 4 — Final Prep (Last 6 Weeks)

Full syllabus revision, JEE Advanced problem sets, exam strategy, slot analysis, and calm-under-pressure techniques.

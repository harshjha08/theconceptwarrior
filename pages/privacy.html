This Privacy Policy explains how The Concept Warriors ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects the personal information of students, parents, and visitors ("you") who interact with our website, forms, WhatsApp communication, or Student LMS.

1 Introduction

We are a personal tutoring service operated by Sandeep Kumar, based in Punjab, India. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to handling your personal data responsibly, transparently, and only for the purposes necessary to provide you with quality educational services.

By using our website (conceptwarriors.in), submitting any form, or enrolling in our programs, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

a) Information You Provide Directly

Form / SourceData Collected
Callback RequestName, phone number, email (optional), preferred time slot
Demo Class RegistrationName, phone number, email, class/grade, target exam
Enrollment FormName, phone number, email, selected course, preferred teaching mode, message/notes

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address (logged when a form is submitted, for spam-prevention purposes only)
  • Browser type, device type, and general usage patterns (via standard web technologies)
  • Theme preference (light/dark mode) and form draft data, stored locally in your browser via localStorage

c) Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect payment card details — all payments are made directly via UPI, bank transfer, or cash, outside this website
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers or sell data to ad networks
  • We do not request sensitive personal data such as religion, caste, or biometric information

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for legitimate educational and business purposes, including to:

  1. Respond to callback requests, demo class bookings, and fee enquiries
  2. Process enrollments and manage your academic records, progress, and test results
  3. Provide access to the Student LMS, recorded lectures, notes, and assignments
  4. Communicate class schedules, doubt-solving support, and academic updates via WhatsApp, phone, or email
  5. Send monthly progress reports to students and parents
  6. Improve our teaching methods, website experience, and service quality
  7. Comply with applicable legal or regulatory requirements

We do not use your personal information for unrelated marketing by third parties, and we do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

4 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Consent — when you voluntarily submit a form or sign up for the Student LMS
  • Contractual necessity — to deliver the tutoring services you have enrolled in and paid for
  • Legitimate interest — to respond to enquiries, improve our services, and prevent spam or misuse

5 Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses your browser's localStorage (not traditional tracking cookies) for the following functional purposes only:

  • Remembering your light/dark theme preference
  • Temporarily storing demo form submissions for the admin dashboard demonstration (where a live backend is not connected)
  • Keeping you logged into the Student LMS or Admin panel during your session

This data is stored locally on your own device and is not transmitted to third-party advertising or analytics networks. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

6 How We Share Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Limited sharing occurs only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third-party tools used to operate our services (e.g., email delivery, video conferencing platforms like Zoom/Google Meet) may process limited data strictly to perform their function.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental request, we may disclose information as legally obligated.
  • Parent/Guardian access: For minor students, progress reports and relevant academic data may be shared with the parent or guardian who enrolled the student.

7 WhatsApp Communication

We use WhatsApp as a primary channel for callback confirmations, doubt-solving, and class updates. By providing your phone number through any form or by initiating a WhatsApp conversation with us, you consent to receiving messages from +91 8427168892 related to your enquiry or enrollment.

WhatsApp messaging is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy and terms, which are outside our control. We recommend reviewing Meta/WhatsApp's privacy practices separately. You may opt out of WhatsApp communication at any time by informing us directly.

8 Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Restricting access to student records and the Admin Dashboard to authorized personnel only (Sandeep Kumar)
  • Using rate-limiting and basic security headers on our backend systems to prevent abuse
  • Storing form submission data in a private, access-controlled database
  • Avoiding storage of sensitive payment information on our systems

While we strive to protect your data using reasonable safeguards, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to promptly addressing any data security concerns brought to our attention.

10 Children's Privacy

Many of our students are minors (under 18). We collect minimal information necessary to provide tutoring services to them, and such information is collected with the knowledge and consent of a parent or guardian who completes the enrollment process.

Parents/guardians may contact us at any time to review, correct, or request deletion of their child's personal information held by us.

11 Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or outdated information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data, subject to any legal retention obligations
  • Withdraw consent — opt out of WhatsApp or email communication at any time
  • Data portability — request your academic records in a reasonable, transferable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 14. We will respond to legitimate requests within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 15–30 days.

12 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms such as WhatsApp, Instagram, YouTube, or video conferencing tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

13 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Significant changes affecting how your data is used will be communicated directly to enrolled students where applicable.
